# Development of the Internet (ARPANET to WWW)
### Introduction 
The internet stands as one of the most remarkable and outstanding evolutions of todays society in the moderna era, the internet is being used by approximately 67.9% of the global population. It's origins trace back to thelate 1960s when the Arpanet was created, a U.S. defense research network designed to enable secure and reliable data sharing between multiple computers at once. Over time, the Arpanet expanded into a global network, laying the groundwork for the internet and, later the WWW ( World Wibe Web).
